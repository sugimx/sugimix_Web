Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sug emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between global surveying technology and local Indian expertise. What started as a small team of passionate professionals has grown into India's most trusted name in precision surveying instruments.

We recognized that Indian surveyors and construction professionals needed access to world-class equipment backed by local support, training, and maintenance services. This insight drove us to establish partnerships with leading global manufacturers while building a robust local infrastructure.
